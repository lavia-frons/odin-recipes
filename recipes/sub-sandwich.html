Shoe Sub

Home

Description

Have an old pair of sneakers that are read to retire?

Then this is the perfect treat you can make for your pooch!

Ingredients

  • Sub roll (french, cuban, hoggie, your call)
  • Worn stinky shoe (the older the better)
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • lunch meat is optional

Directions

  1. Smother inside of bread with mayo and mustard
  2. Layer lettuce, tomatoes and pickles
  3. Optional: Stuff shoe with lunch meat
  4. Place shoe between bread and smoosh close
