Puppy Chow

Home

Description

A sweet and salty treat you will love.

Although it's called puppy chow, this treat contains chocolate and shouldn't be shared with pups. Sorry, Fido.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Peanut Butter
  • Rice Chex Cereal
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Melt peanut buter and chocolate
  2. Stir melted mixture and coat the cereal
  3. Coat in powerded sugar
  4. Chill
